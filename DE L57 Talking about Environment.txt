subject:DE L57 Talking about Environment
dialog:
A: Don’t throw the batteries into the garbage basket. 
B: Why?
A: Don’t you know that the battery is polluting? 
B: I have no idea.
A: It should be collected separately. There is a special box for it at the entrance of this department. Take them there.
B: I see. When did you become an environmentalist?
A: I’m not. Everybody should pay attention to the environmental protection. 
B: I can’t agree with you anymore.
A: We need to enhance our sense of environmental protection. 
B: Absolutely.
Are you a person with sense of environmental protection?
