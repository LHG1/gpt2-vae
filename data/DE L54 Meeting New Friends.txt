A: Hi! My name is Peter, what’s your name? 
B: I’m Jane. Nice to meet you.
A: It’s my pleasure. This is a great party! 
B: Yes, it is. Where are you from?
A: I’m from Seoul.
B: Seoul? So you are Japanese.
A: No, I’m not Japanese. I’m Korean.
B: Oh, you are Korean. Sorry about that. My geography lesson’s a mess at school.
A: That’s ok. Where are you from?
B: I’m from London. Excuse me, which city are you from? 
A: Seoul, capital of South Korea.
B: Oh, got it. So you speak Korean, right? 
A: Yes, most residents do.
