subject:DE L46 Talking about Jobs
dialog:
A: Hi! How are you?
B: Very good! What about you? 
A: Great! May I know your name?
B: My name is Jack. What’s your job? 
A: I am an office worker. And you?
B: I work as a sales manager.
A: I want to introduce my sister to you.
B: I would love to meet her. What does she do for a living? 
A: She is a hairdresser.
B: Do you like your job?
A: Not really, it’s too tiring. The pressure builds up every day. 
B: It doesn’t feel good.
A: Yeah. The pressure is way too much, so I quit my job.
