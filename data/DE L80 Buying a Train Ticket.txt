subject:DE L80 Buying a Train Ticket
dialog:
A: What time is the next train to New York? 
B: It’s T945 at 9:26 pm.
A: When does it get there?
B: It gets there at 10:00 am tomorrow. 
A: How much is the ticket?
B: Sleeper or hard seat? 
A: I prefer hard seat.
B: It’s $80 one way or $140 round trip.
A: A one way ticket please.
B: Here you are. Have a nice trip.
