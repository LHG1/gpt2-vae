subject:DE L52 Birthday
dialog:
A: It’s my birthday this Saturday. Are you available on Saturday? 
B: I guess.
A: I’d like you to come to my party.
B: Thanks for your invitation. What are you going to do?
A: I’m going to invite some close friends over to my house and play some board games.
B: When and where is the party?
A: Saturday night at 8 in my apartment.
B: That’s great. Who else will come to the party? 
A: Just some of my co-workers.
B: I’m not quite sure because I will be busy working that day. I will try to make it to your party.
