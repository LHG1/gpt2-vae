A: Hi, I’m Jack. I’m from England. What’s your name?

A: It is very nice to meet you.

B: I’m a singer.
A: Cool. I like singing, but I’m not good at that.
B: I can teach you if you have time. 
A: Really? That will be great!
B: Let’s fix time to do it.
